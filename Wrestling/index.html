Wrestling

I got into wrestling because my cousins told me that I would really enjoy it throughout highschool, as I can get lots of exercise and make friends along the way. I love the times when we got to wrestle eachother as it shows how far we have gotten with our skills. Wrestling is a contact sport that allows you to compete with people nation/world wide and see how many people also have a big passion for such an amazing sport.

I have many interests when it come to wrestling, as I watch the professional wrestle i see that they are able to pull off crazy moves that I have never seen before. These guys that wrestle show how much our body can do and how we can use different technique to take a oppenent down.
